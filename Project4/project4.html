Welcome to spot the difference!!!!! (*≧ω≦*)

How to play!!

- There are 3 levels! Easy, Medium, and Hard.

- Use your mouse and hover over the images.

- Spot the difference between these photos!

- Try your best and have fun!

Easy

You have 10 seconds to view the images. Remove your mouse from the image once the time is up.

Medium

You have 5 seconds to view the images. Remove your mouse from the image once the time is up.

Hard

You have 3 seconds to view the images. Remove your mouse from the image once the time is up.
